SIMPLE COUSCOUS RECEIP:

Couscous

  • the ingredients:

  • 300g lamb neck
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 turnips
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste

steps to do:

  • 1- In a casserole dish, brown the chicken thighs and lamb neck, previously cut into pieces, with a tablespoon of olive oil. Set aside. Clean the zucchini, carrots, turnips, and celery. Peel the carrots and turnips. Peel the celery stalk. Cut all the vegetables into chunks. Peel and slice the onion. In a casserole dish, sweat it with a tablespoon of olive oil. Add the vegetables and saut√© for a few minutes. Stir in the ras el hanout, tomato paste, and chicken stock cube. Add the chicken thighs and lamb pieces. Season with salt. Mix and cover with water. Simmer, covered, over low heat for 45 minutes. Drain and rinse the chickpeas. Add them to the casserole dish 10 minutes before the end of cooking time. Adjust the seasoning if necessary and sprinkle with chopped cilantro. In a very hot pan, without adding any fat, grill the merguez sausages. Set aside
  • 2- Semolina In a large bowl, combine two cups of couscous grains. Add salt and olive oil. Fluff with a fork before adding two cups of boiling water. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff again with a fork.
  • 3- Serving Arrange the chicken thighs, lamb pieces, and merguez sausages on a platter. Serve with couscous and vegetables in their broth.
